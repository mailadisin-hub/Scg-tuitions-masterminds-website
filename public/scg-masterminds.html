About SCG Masterminds

What is SCG Masterminds?

At SCG Masterminds, we provide engaging and effective Maths and English tuition for children in KS1 and Year 3. Our carefully structured programme follows the National Curriculum, helping pupils strengthen their understanding of key concepts while developing confidence in the classroom.

Lessons are delivered in small groups by experienced tutors, ensuring every child receives individual attention and support. Through interactive activities, targeted teaching and regular assessment, we help children build essential skills, close learning gaps and reach their full potential.

To reinforce learning, pupils receive weekly homework that complements the topics covered in class and encourages independent practice at home. Our supportive and nurturing environment enables children to develop strong academic foundations, positive study habits and a lifelong love of learning.

Whether your child needs extra support, wants to boost their confidence, or is aiming to excel, SCG Masterminds provides the guidance and encouragement they need to succeed.
